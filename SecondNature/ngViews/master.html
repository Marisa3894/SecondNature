Our Zafu, Zabouton and Mini-Zab meditation pillows are carefully crafted, using beautiful hemp and hemp blend fabrics and cotton or buckwheat hull fillings to embody the benefits of natural, organic and locally grown materials.



Zafus & Zaboutons

Uniquely beautiful covers are created from organically grown hemp, flax, silk, cotton in a variety of combinations. All fabrics are woven and dyed in the US, and contribute to the support and development of local farming, weaving and manufacturing communities.

The buckwheat hull Neck Roll and Neck Pillows are designed to support the neck and provide relief for neck, back pain, and even snoring. The preflattened buckwheat hull filling maintains its volume and does not compress during the night.
